Understanding the points system

In this story, there are three main point categories that will influence the ending:

  • Conflict Points: These represent emotional distance, conflict, or antagonism between Bakugo and Deku. The higher the Tension, the more likely the ending will be bad.
  • Friendship Points: These represent the bond between Bakugo and Deku, based on mutual respect and cooperation. A higher Friendship score will lead to a neutral ending.
  • Affection Points: These reflect the deeper, emotional connection between Bakugo and Deku. A higher Affection score will lead to a good ending.

The highest point category will determine the outcome of the story.

Pathway Hints for Each Ending:

Bad Ending (Conflict is the highest):
  • Key Indicators: If you consistently ignore or belittle Deku, show excessive pride, or have heated confrontations, Tension will rise, leading to a Bad Ending.
  • Path to Avoid: Confronting Deku in an overly aggressive manner, pushing him away, or making emotionally charged decisions without reconciliation will likely lead to the Bad Ending.
  • Tip: Keep an eye on your emotional distance. If you feel like Bakugo is not allowing Deku to grow or pushing him away too much, you're on the path to Tension.
Neutral Ending (Friendship is the highest):
  • Key Indicators: If you focus on mutual respect and cooperation but avoid fully exploring deeper emotional connections, the Friendship points will be highest, leading to a Neutral Ending.
  • Path to Follow: Working together, acknowledging each otherâ€™s strengths, or taking time to bond can lead to a solid friendship, but not necessarily a romantic or deeply emotional connection.
  • Tip: If Bakugo respects Deku but doesnâ€™t let down his guard or open up too much, Friendship will grow, leading to a Neutral Ending.
Good Ending (Affection is the highest):
  • Key Indicators: If Bakugoâ€™s actions show vulnerability, trust, and emotional connection toward Deku, the Affection points will lead to a Good Ending.
  • Path to Follow: Sharing quiet moments, supporting Deku in emotional or life-threatening situations, and showing vulnerability in the right context will strengthen the emotional bond.
  • Tip: Watch for moments where Bakugo shows genuine care for Deku, especially when he puts his guard down and shares something meaningful. These moments foster Affection and lead to the Good Ending.
